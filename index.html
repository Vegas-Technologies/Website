Over 15 Years of Service

Leading Techs delivers Event IT Support services for conferences, trade shows, summits, expos, showcases, and webcasts. We offer robust networking setups and deployment — from basic configurations to enterprise-grade infrastructures. Our team ensures seamless connectivity through Ethernet, fiber installation and termination, wireless, and satellite solutions, providing reliable internet access tailored to each event’s needs.

Our Speaker-Ready and Breakout Services ensure presenters and virtual speakers have smooth, stress-free experiences with IT setup, rehearsals, slide management, and real-time technical support. At Leading Techs, we combine technical expertise, a commitment to excellence, and unparalleled event support to provide reliable solutions that exceed expectations — whether it’s for permanent infrastructures or high-impact events.
