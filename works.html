About Us

We offer several different types of careers

(moved from index) Leading Techs is a privately held Computer Networking and IT company founded in 2013, headquartered in Las Vegas, NV. We combine years of expertise in Networking, WiFi, Security, and Low Voltage Cabling to deliver complete network solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

Would you enjoy completing IT projects for clients that range from $30k to $2M? Do you want to gain more experience with Project Management and Implementations? Would you like to learn how to configure networking equipment from multiple vendors including Cisco, HP, Ruckus, and Motorola? If so, then take a look at the careers Leading Techs has to offer below.

Network Implementation Engineer

In this position you would have the opportunity to travel around the United States completing these projects with all travel expenses paid. We are looking for entry-level or mid-level Network Technicians with a current networking certification. If you are interested please send us an e-mail with your resume and a paragraph explaining why you would love this job.

Data Cabler

In this position you would have the opportunity to travel around the United States completing these projects with all travel expenses paid. We are seeking BICSI certified cabling intallers. Little to no experience is ok, as you will be working with a team of experts. If you are interested please send us an e-mail with your resume and a paragraph explaining why you would love this job.

Contracting Jobs

In this position you would have the opportunity to work on and complete small to large enterprise projects. The contracting jobs we offer depend on the type and size of the project. You would most likely be working as a data cabler for physical layer network installations. If you are interested please send us an e-mail with your resume and a paragraph explaining why you would love this job.
